REMEDY FOR PARKINSON'S DISEASE

Patients have to take foods like

  • Take antioxidants food like nuts,berries
  • Take nightshade vegetables(tomatos,peppers and eggplant),leafy green vegetables
  • Take fava beans because they contain levodopa

Patients have to avoid foods like

  • Food high in saturated fat(cheese,butter,some baked and fried foods)
  • Foods that are hard to chew
  • Avoid processed foods such as canned foods,fried foods and diet sodas

Lifestyle and home remedies

  • Drink plenty of water
  • Spend time outside
  • Various kinds of exercise and physical therapy
  • Talk to your doctor about supplements and other therapies
